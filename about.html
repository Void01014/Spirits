Who are we?

We are the Spirits , a group united by a single principle: transparency.. Each member came from different paths, but we all share a desire to make student voices heard clearly and honestly. Like pieces of a prism, we've come together to form a whole that reflects every perspective without distortion.


Our mission is simple:

✨ Our mission is simple but powerful: we serve as a bridge between students and the administration. We are a see-through mirror, reflecting the students’ thoughts, ideas, and concerns while staying true to their voices. Through us, the light of every student can pass through freely, shaping a school community where everyone feels seen, heard, and represented.
